# Tenant Guide

Welcome to ArrendaOco! This guide will help you navigate the platform as a tenant looking for your ideal rental property in Ocosingo, Chiapas.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

To start searching for properties, you'll need to create an account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Registration Page">
    Navigate to the registration page at `/registro` or click "Registrarse" from the homepage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Out Your Information">
    Provide the following required information:
    - **Full Name**: Your complete name
    - **Email Address**: A valid email you have access to
    - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters
    - **Password Confirmation**: Re-enter your password
    - **Profile Photo** (Optional): Upload a profile picture (max 2MB)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Alternative: Social Login">
    You can also register quickly using your Google account by clicking the "Google" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Click "Registrarse" to create your account. You'll be automatically logged in and assigned the "Inquilino" (Tenant) role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
All new users are automatically assigned the Tenant role, allowing immediate access to search and view properties.
</Note>

### Logging In

If you already have an account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page">
    Click "Iniciar sesión" or navigate to `/login`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide your email and password, or use Google login
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access the Platform">
    You'll be redirected to the home page (`/inicio`) with available properties
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Recovery

If you forget your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot Password'">
    On the login page, click "¿Olvidaste tu contraseña?"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide the email address associated with your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Your Inbox">
    You'll receive an email with a password reset link (single-use token with limited validity)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Click the link in the email and enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Searching for Properties

### Browsing Available Properties

The home page displays the latest available properties:

- **9 properties per page** with pagination
- Only properties with status "disponible" (available) are shown
- Each property card shows:
  - Main photo
  - Title and address
  - Property type (casa, departamento, cuarto)
  - Monthly rent amount
  - Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
  - Square meters
  - Heart icon to add to favorites

### Using Search Filters

Navigate to `/buscar` to access advanced search filters:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter by Location">
    Enter keywords in the "Ubicación" field to search by:
    - Property title
    - Street address
    - City name (Ocosingo)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Property Type">
    Choose from:
    - Casa (House)
    - Departamento (Apartment)
    - Cuarto (Room)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Price Range">
    Select your budget range:
    - $0 - $2,000 MXN
    - $2,000 - $4,000 MXN
    - $4,000 - $6,000 MXN
    - $6,000+ MXN
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Results update automatically and display 12 properties per page
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Property Details

Click on any property card to view complete information:

- **Photo Gallery**: All uploaded images of the property
- **Property Details**:
  - Full description
  - Exact address and location on interactive map
  - Property type
  - Number of bedrooms (habitaciones)
  - Number of bathrooms (baños)
  - Square meters (metros)
  - Monthly rent (renta mensual)
  - Required deposit (depósito)
- **Video Tour**: YouTube video if available
- **Landlord Information**: Name and contact details
- **Reviews**: Ratings and comments from previous tenants

<Tip>
Use the interactive map to verify the exact location and proximity to your workplace, schools, or other important locations.
</Tip>

## Managing Favorites

### Adding Properties to Favorites

Keep track of properties you're interested in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Heart Icon">
    On any property card or detail page, click the heart icon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Property Saved">
    The property is immediately added to your favorites list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Notes (Optional)">

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Your Favorites

Access all your saved properties:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Favorites">
    Click on "Favoritos" in the navigation menu or go to `/favoritos`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Your List">
    See all properties you've marked as favorites with full details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Notes">
    Update or edit your personal notes for each property using the edit button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove Favorites">
    Click the heart icon again to remove properties from your favorites
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contacting Landlords

When you find a property you like:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Property Details">
    Open the complete property listing
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Landlord Information">
    The property owner's (propietario) name is displayed on the detail page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reach Out">
    Contact the landlord directly to:
    - Schedule a property viewing
    - Ask questions about the property
    - Discuss rental terms
    - Submit your rental application
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
ArrendaOco facilitates property discovery. Rental agreements and contracts are managed through the system, but initial contact and viewings should be arranged directly with the landlord.
</Warning>

If you want to list your own properties:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Profile">

## Rental Contracts & Payments

### Understanding Contracts

Once you agree to rent a property with a landlord:

- A digital contract (contrato) will be created linking:
  - You (inquilino/tenant)
  - The landlord (propietario)
  - The specific property (inmueble)
- Contract includes:
  - Start and end dates
  - Monthly rent amount
  - Required deposit
  - Property status changes to "Rentado" (Rented)

### Managing Payments

The system automatically handles rent tracking:

- **Monthly payment records** are generated automatically for the contract duration
- Each payment includes:
  - Base rent amount
  - Due date
  - Payment status (Pendiente/Completado)

### Making Payments

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Payment Due">
    Access your contract details to see upcoming payments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Make your payment through the agreed method with your landlord
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Payment Registration">
    Once processed, the payment status changes to "Completado" with:
    - Payment date
    - Reference number
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Late payments may incur automatic late fees (recargos). The system compares the due date with the current date and adds configured penalties to overdue amounts.
</Warning>

### Viewing Payment History

Access your financial records:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Contract Details">
    Navigate to your active contract
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Statement of Account">
    See chronological list of:
    - All payments made
    - Outstanding balances
    - Late fees applied
    - Total balance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Records">
    Generate PDF or Excel reports of your payment history
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Leaving Reviews

### When You Can Review

Share your rental experience after your lease:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Your Rental">
    You can only review properties where you had an active contract
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Property">

  </Step>

After posting a review:

- **Edit**: Update your rating or comment if your opinion changes

Honest reviews help other tenants make informed decisions. Mention specifics about maintenance, communication with landlord, neighborhood safety, and property condition.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Invalid credentials error

**Solutions**:
- Verify email and password are correct
- Check if your account status is "Activo" (contact admin if suspended)
- Use password recovery if forgotten
- Try social login if you registered with Google

### Property Not Showing in Search

**Problem**: Can't find a specific property

**Solutions**:
- Verify property status is "disponible" (not "rentado" or deleted)
- Adjust search filters - they may be too restrictive
- Check if property matches your price range
- Browse all properties from home page instead of filtered search

### Can't Add to Favorites

**Problem**: Heart icon not responding

**Solutions**:
- Ensure you're logged in (favorites require authentication)
- Refresh the page and try again
- Check browser console for errors

### Video Not Playing

**Problem**: YouTube video not loading on property page

**Solutions**:
- Verify the landlord uploaded a valid YouTube URL
- Check your internet connection
- Try opening the video link directly on YouTube
- Some properties may not have video tours

### Can't Submit Review

**Problem**: Review submission fails

**Solutions**:
- Confirm you had a rental contract for this property
- Check if you already reviewed this property (no duplicates allowed)
- Ensure both rating and comment are provided
- Verify you're logged in

## Privacy & Safety

### Your Data Protection

- All passwords are encrypted using BCrypt hashing
- Platform uses HTTPS for secure data transmission
- Personal information (phone, address) is not exposed to unauthorized users
- Only landlords and administrators can see full contact details after formal interaction

### Safe Rental Practices

<Warning>
Always:
- Visit properties in person before committing
- Verify landlord identity and property ownership
- Read contracts carefully before signing
- Keep records of all payments and communications
- Never send money before viewing a property
</Warning>
